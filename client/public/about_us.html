About Us

Team 3J

Group of motivated students studying computer science at Radford University.... Our team likes to keep things simple, hence our name. We came up with the team name based on the sole fact that all of our team members have first names that start with 'J'. Our goal was to create a simple and fun software that anyone can use. We came up with the idea of an all-in-one responsive board that users can customize for their liking using drag and drop elements. This software is our teams first attempt at a relatively large scale engineering project. We hope to continue our efforts and studies at Radford University so that we may find more inspiration to tackle more project such as this.
